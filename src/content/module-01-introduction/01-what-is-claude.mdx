# What is Claude?

Claude is a family of large language models (LLMs) developed by **Anthropic**, an AI safety company founded in 2021. Unlike many AI models built primarily for performance benchmarks, Claude is designed with a core focus on being **helpful, harmless, and honest**.

## The Company Behind Claude

Anthropic was founded by former members of OpenAI, including Dario Amodei and Daniela Amodei. The company's mission is to develop AI systems that are safe, reliable, and interpretable — placing safety research at the center of everything they build.

> **Fun fact:** The name "Claude" was chosen as a friendly, approachable name — a nod to Claude Shannon, the father of information theory.

## What Can Claude Do?

Claude is a general-purpose AI assistant capable of:

- **Writing & editing** — drafting emails, essays, stories, and more
- **Coding** — writing, debugging, and explaining code in any language
- **Analysis** — summarizing documents, extracting insights, and reasoning through data
- **Research** — answering complex questions with nuanced, accurate responses
- **Conversation** — engaging in thoughtful, multi-turn dialogue

## Constitutional AI

One of Anthropic's core innovations is **Constitutional AI (CAI)** — a training approach that teaches Claude to be helpful and harmless using a set of principles (a "constitution") rather than relying solely on human feedback for every edge case. This makes Claude's safety properties more robust and consistent.
